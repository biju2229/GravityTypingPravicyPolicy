# Privacy Policy for Gravity Typing

**Last updated: March 2, 2026**

Gravity Typing ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the following types of information:
* **Account Information:** Email address and password used for registration and login (via Firebase Authentication).
* **Usage Data:** Typing test scores, word efficiency results, time taken, and exam history stored to track your progress.
* **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, and app version for debugging and compatibility.
* **Subscription Info:** Subscription status and expiry date stored in Firebase Firestore.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* To provide personalized typing practice and track your progress over time.
* To manage your account, subscription, and access permissions.
* To improve app features and fix bugs based on usage patterns.
* To send important account-related notifications (e.g., subscription expiry).

## 3. Data Storage & Security
Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services (Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore). We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS). However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 4. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services that may collect data as per their own privacy policies:
* **Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore)** — [firebase.google.com/support/privacy](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)
* **Google Play Services** — [policies.google.com/privacy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## 5. Data Sharing
We do NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your data is only shared with Firebase/Google as required for app functionality.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Gravity Typing is designed as an educational typing tool suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us immediately.

## 7. Your Rights
* Request deletion of your account and all associated data.
* Access the personal data we hold about you.
* Correct inaccurate data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

## 8. Retention
We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Inactive or expired accounts are automatically deleted after 5 days of inactivity beyond the subscription period. Typing results and progress data are retained until you delete your account.

## 9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
