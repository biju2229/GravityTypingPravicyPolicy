Welcome to Gravity Typing. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

We collect the following information when you use our app:

We use the information we collect to:

Your data is stored securely using Firebase (Google's cloud platform). We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

5. Google Sign-In

Our app uses Google Sign-In for authentication. By signing in, you agree to Google's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. We only access your basic profile information (name and email) and do not access any other Google account data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is designed for use by individuals of all ages, including students learning Hindi typing. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.
